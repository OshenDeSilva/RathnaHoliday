About Rathna Tours

Your trusted travel partner in Sri Lanka since 2005

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Rathna Tours began as a small family business with a passion for showcasing the incredible beauty and rich culture of Sri Lanka. What started as a modest operation with just one vehicle has grown into a respected tour company known for exceptional service and authentic experiences.

Today, we continue to operate as a family business, maintaining the personal touch that has always set us apart. Our team includes experienced guides, skilled drivers, and travel experts who all share a deep love for our country and a commitment to creating unforgettable journeys for our guests.

We believe that travel should be transformative, and we're dedicated to creating experiences that not only show you the highlights of Sri Lanka but also give you a genuine connection to its people, culture, and natural wonders.

Our Services

Comprehensive travel solutions for your Sri Lankan journey

Customized Itineraries

We create personalized travel plans based on your interests, schedule, and budget. Whether you're seeking cultural immersion, adventure, relaxation, or a mix of everything, we'll design the perfect Sri Lankan experience for you.

Transportation

Travel in comfort and safety with our modern, well-maintained vehicles. Our experienced drivers know Sri Lanka's roads intimately and will ensure you reach each destination smoothly and efficiently.

Accommodation

From luxury resorts to charming guesthouses, we partner with the best accommodations across Sri Lanka. We carefully select properties that offer comfort, character, and authentic Sri Lankan hospitality.

Expert Guides

Our knowledgeable local guides bring Sri Lanka's history, culture, and natural wonders to life. Fluent in multiple languages, they're passionate about sharing their insights and ensuring you have a meaningful travel experience.

Ticket Reservations

We handle all entrance tickets, permits, and activity bookings in advance, saving you time and ensuring seamless access to attractions, national parks, and cultural sites throughout your journey.

Culinary Experiences

Discover the flavors of Sri Lanka with curated food experiences. From street food tours to cooking classes with local families, we'll introduce you to the diverse and delicious cuisine of our island.
